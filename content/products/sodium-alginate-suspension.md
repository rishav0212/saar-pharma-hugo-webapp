## Mechanism of Action
The combination works via a dual-action process:
1. **Barrier Formation**: Sodium Alginate forms a resilient gel raft.
2. **Neutralization**: Sodium Bicarbonate and Calcium Carbonate neutralize excess acid while releasing CO2 to float the raft.

## Drug Interactions & Safety
