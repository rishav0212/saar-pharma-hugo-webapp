## Mechanism of Action & Formulation Rationale {#mechanism}

Each ingredient in this spray serves a distinct pharmacological or formulation function:

**Diclofenac Diethylamine (1.16% or 2.32% w/w)** inhibits COX-1 and COX-2 enzymes, suppressing prostaglandin synthesis at the site of pain and inflammation. The Diethylamine salt form provides markedly better transdermal penetration than sodium-based alternatives, concentrating the drug in the target tissue with minimal systemic absorption.

**Methyl Salicylate (10% w/w)** produces localised hyperaemia via rubefacient action, increasing blood flow to the injured tissue and accelerating resolution of inflammatory metabolites.

**Menthol (5% w/w)** activates TRPM8 thermoreceptors, delivering rapid cooling sensation that serves both as a counterirritant and a perceptual analgesic — a strong patient experience driver for brand recall.

**Linseed Oil (3% w/w)** functions as an omega-3 rich emollient base with intrinsic anti-inflammatory properties, reducing skin irritation during prolonged therapeutic use.

**Benzyl Alcohol (1% w/w)** enhances permeation of the active actives through the stratum corneum while serving as an antimicrobial stabiliser for the formulation matrix.

Together, these actives create a multi-mechanism product that addresses both the biochemical and sensory dimensions of pain — a combination that is highly differentiated in the topical pain segment.
