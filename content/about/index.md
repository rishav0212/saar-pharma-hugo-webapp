# About Saar Biotech

## A Legacy of Quality and Trust Since 2005
Saar Biotech Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between high-end pharmaceutical research and accessible manufacturing. Starting in 2005 in the industrial hub of Baddi, Himachal Pradesh, we have evolved from a single-line production facility into a multi-unit industrial powerhouse with over 20 years of manufacturing focus.

## State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Infrastructure
Our facility at Plot No. 125-126, EPIP, Phase-1, Baddi, is the heart of our operations. It is a WHO-GMP and ISO certified unit designed to meet the most stringent global standards.

- **Specialized Dermatology Section**: High-capacity production for ointments, creams, and gels.
- **Sterile Ophthalmology Unit**: Advanced sterile filling for eye and ear drops.
- **Oral Liquid Production**: Sophisticated lines for syrups and suspensions.

## Our Core Philosophy: Quality First
At Saar Biotech, quality is not just a certification—it is a culture. Our in-house GLP-compliant labs ensure that every batch of product meets the precise specifications required by our marketing partners.
