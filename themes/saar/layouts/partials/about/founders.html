Founded in 2005 by Mr. Radhe Sham Goyal and Mr. Anil Kumar, Saar Biotech was built on the foundation of technical expertise and ethical manufacturing. Our founders envisioned a facility that could serve the world's leading pharma brands with the precision of a boutique laboratory and the scale of an industrial giant.

Today, that vision has materialized into a multi-unit operation in Baddi, Himachal Pradesh, where we combine two decades of experience with modern automation to deliver safety and stability in every dosage.
