Liquid Manufacturing

Liquid Dosage Manufacturing Requirements

For syrups, oral suspensions, and drop formats — share formulation type, packing size, quantity, and any stability or taste requirements.

  • Approved composition or formula name
  • Dosage form and concentration
  • Pack size, cap type, bottle material
  • Expected quantity (MOQ and repeat)
  • Target timeline
Helpful brief Mention formulation type, pack size, and quantity in your enquiry for faster response.
Topical Products

Topical & Derma Product Requirements

For ointments, gels, creams, and sprays — mention application area, base type, preferred packing, and any regulatory market requirements.

  • Product type: ointment, cream, gel, spray
  • Active ingredient and base
  • Tube, jar, or pump packing preference
  • Pack size and branding requirements
  • Export or domestic market
Helpful brief Topical enquiries move faster with base type, packing format, and target market included.

Private Label Manufacturing Requirements

For brand-ready private label supply — share your product list, dosage preferences, artwork readiness, and repeat order planning details.

  • Documentation needs (COA, batch records)

What does Saar Biotech manufacture for third-party pharma companies?

Saar Biotech manufactures approved pharmaceutical dosage forms such as syrups, suspensions, oral liquids, drops, ointments, creams, gels, topical solutions, and sprays for pharma marketing companies and healthcare brands.

Does Saar Biotech support private-label pharma manufacturing?
